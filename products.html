Glass & Plasticwares

Bottles

Used for storing, mixing, or transporting liquids in laboratory settings, often with secure caps.

Adapters

Connect different glassware components, allowing transitions between sizes or joint types.

Beakers

Cylindrical glass vessels with a flat bottom, used for stirring, mixing, and heating liquids.

Funnels

Used to channel liquids or fine-grained substances into containers with small openings.

Containers

General-purpose vessels for holding solids, liquids, or samples in the lab.

Condensers

Cool hot vapors back into liquid form during distillation or reflux processes.

Desiccators

Airtight chambers used to keep samples dry by removing moisture with desiccants.

Joints

Precision-ground glass connectors that join lab glassware pieces securely.

Stopcocks

Valve devices used to control the flow of liquids or gases in tubing or glassware.

Racks

Support frames designed to hold test tubes, pipettes, or other lab items upright.

Sample Handling

Tools and containers used for preparing, storing, or transferring lab samples.

Thermometers

Instruments used to measure temperature, often in Celsius or Fahrenheit, for lab experiments.

SVL Glassware with Screwthread Joints

Glassware featuring threaded joints for secure, leak-proof connections without ground glass.

Stirrers

Tools or devices used to agitate liquids, ensuring proper mixing or reaction.

Lab Equipments

Ovens

Used for drying, sterilizing, or heat-treating samples at controlled temperatures in laboratory environments.

Chiller and Freezer

Equipment for preserving samples or reagents by maintaining low or sub-zero temperatures.

Balances

Precision instruments used to measure the mass of substances with high accuracy.

Test Sieves

Mesh-based tools used to separate particles by size for material analysis and grading.

Hotplate & Stirrers

Combine surface heating with magnetic stirring to mix and heat solutions simultaneously..

Viscometers

Instruments used to measure the viscosity or flow resistance of liquids..

Temperature Control Devices

Equipment designed to precisely regulate heating or cooling in laboratory processes (e.g., water baths, incubators, chillers).

Centrifuge

A device that spins samples at high speeds to separate components based on density..

Mixing Devices

Instruments used to blend, stir, or agitate solutions for consistent chemical or sample preparation.
